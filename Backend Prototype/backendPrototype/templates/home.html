Funding sources and requests for proposals resources:

SPIN

Searchable database of over 40,000 opportunities from more that 10,000 global sponsors. Use SPIN to search for research grants, fellowships, training and various other funding opportunities. Access funding information online, from any browser. Organize grant information in one convenient place.

Set up your account and start your search for funding.

Foundation center

Provide numerous electronic resources to assist in finding funding opportunities and developing grant proposals.

Start your search in the Foundation Center today.

Grants.gov

Offers the ability to search for Federal government-wide grant opportunities. Users can search by full text, by date, by CFDA number, by funding topic, and by agency. users can also register to receive grant notifications by e-mail, under several options.

See what Grants.gov has to offer.

Philanthropy News Digest RFP Bulletin

Maintained by the Foundation Center, the Philanthropy News Digest RFP Bulletin is revised each week. The web site also includes an option for a weekly e-mail update.
